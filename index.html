WELCOME

Meta Foxonry is a collection of Meta Foxes ruling the world while living on the NEAR blockchain.

By holding Meta Fox, you own something more significant than a hyped PFP. Each Meta Fox NFT acts as a unique access key to the Meta Foxonry services ecosystem.

Every single Meta Fox NFT is stakable and upgradable. It's crucial since you'll be able to earn NEAR from your NFTs while holding them.

OUR MISSION

We have two primary missions: to develop NEAR ecosystem and provide value for our holders.

The main issue of the NEAR NFT market is the lack of liquidity caused by its youth. In simple words, liquidity means the ability of the community to buy something. Higher liquidity - higher floor prices, more sales, increased quality of the projects.

So, our mission is to help the NEAR NFT ecosystem by increasing its liquidity. Every single service we're building is designed to boost the entire ecosystem and share its profits with holders.

STAKING & UPGRADING

We have a quite simple hierarchy system of our Foxes. The income and privileges of NFT depend on its level.

Common Fox is the most usual ones. We'll have about 70% of them in our supply.

Wadget Foxes are the next step of evolution. Wadgets sign will appear on absolutely random Foxes with a 30% chance. If you'll notice that your Fox has a third holographic eye on its head - our congrats!

Magister Foxes are the last level of Fox evolution. You'll need to burn 3 Wadget Foxes and some $MFOX tokens to get one. But don't hurry up to burn them by yourself! We'll have a special website for this.

Staking is the only way to mint our functional token called $MFOX. We believe staking is an essential feature for any valuable collection because it'll allow holders to earn from their NFTs without selling them. Also, it has several utilities to keep supply and demand balanced. The elaborate tokenomics and the huge number of ways to use them guarantee its systematic growing and support the good level of income from staking.
